Programs That Create Change

Each program is community-led, rigorously tracked, and designed for long-term impact.

📚 Education 🟢 Active

Vidya Jyoti — Lighting Up Young Minds

Vidya Jyoti ("Light of Knowledge") is our flagship education program, providing free tutoring, school supplies, digital literacy training, and mentorship to children aged 6–16 from low-income families in rural Telangana and Andhra Pradesh.

We operate weekend learning centers in 12 villages, staffed by trained volunteer teachers. Our curriculum supplements the government school system with foundational literacy, numeracy, and life skills — ensuring no child falls through the cracks.

🍱 Nutrition 🟢 Active

Annadaan — No One Sleeps Hungry

Annadaan ("Gift of Food") is our community nutrition program combating hunger and food insecurity among urban poor, migrant workers, and homeless individuals in Hyderabad, Pune, and Bengaluru.

Every Sunday, our volunteer teams operate community kitchens, distributing freshly prepared, nutritious meals along with ration kits for families. During emergencies (floods, COVID), we scale up to deliver 1,000+ meals per day.

🌿 Environment 🟢 Active

HaritaBhoomi — Greening Our Mother Earth

HaritaBhoomi ("Green Earth") is our environmental flagships program driving large-scale tree plantation drives, river clean-up campaigns, plastic-free awareness, and biodiversity conservation in urban and peri-urban areas.

Each sapling is geo-tagged, monitored for 1 year post-planting, and maintained by local community members who receive environmental stewardship training. Our 85% survival rate far exceeds national averages.

❤️ Health 🔵 Seasonal

Aarogya Seva — Free Health Camps

Through Aarogya Seva ("Health Service"), we organize quarterly free health check-up camps in villages and urban slums, offering basic diagnostics, doctor consultations, medicines, and mental health awareness sessions.

Our medical volunteers — doctors, nurses, and health workers — provide services typically inaccessible to marginalized communities, including eye check-ups, dental care, and prenatal consultations.
