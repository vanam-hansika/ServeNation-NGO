FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about volunteering and partnerships.

How do I become a volunteer?

Simply fill out our Volunteer Registration Form. Our team will review your application and contact you within 48 hours to schedule an orientation call.

Can I volunteer remotely?

Yes! We have remote volunteer roles in content creation, social media management, graphic design, data analysis, grant writing, and online tutoring. Specify your preference in the registration form.

How can my company partnership or CSR collaboration?

We welcome corporate partnerships for CSR programs, employee volunteering days, event sponsorships, and project co-funding. Email us at partnerships@servenation.org.

Is ServeNation a registered NGO?

Yes, ServeNation is a legally registered Society under the Societies Registration Act, 1860, and holds 80G and 12A tax exemption certificates. Donations are eligible for 50% tax deductions under Section 80G.
